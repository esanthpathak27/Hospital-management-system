Doctor's Dashboard

Schedule Management

- View appointments scheduled for the day, week, or month.
- Add, edit, or cancel appointments.
- Set reminders for upcoming appointments.

Patient History

- Access detailed patient records including medical history, diagnoses, treatments, and medications.
- Search and filter patient records based on various criteria such as name, date of birth, or medical condition.
- View lab test results and diagnostic reports.

Laboratory Test Management

- Order laboratory tests for patients directly from the dashboard.
- Track the status of pending, in-progress, and completed lab tests.
- Receive notifications when test results are available.

Patient Communication

- Communicate with patients securely through messaging or video conferencing.
- Send reminders for upcoming appointments or follow-up consultations.
- Provide educational resources and instructions for patients regarding lab tests and procedures.

Analytics and Reporting

- Generate reports on patient demographics, appointment statistics, and test utilization.
- Analyze trends in patient health outcomes and treatment efficacy.
- Identify areas for improvement in healthcare delivery and resource allocation.

Integration with Electronic Health Records (EHR)

- Seamlessly integrate with existing EHR systems to access comprehensive patient information.
- Ensure data consistency and interoperability between different healthcare systems and providers.

Security and Privacy Controls

- Implement robust security measures to protect patient data and comply with HIPAA or other regulatory requirements.
- Encrypt sensitive information and enforce access controls based on user roles and permissions.

Customization and Personalization

- Allow doctors to customize their dashboard layout, preferences, and favorite shortcuts.
- Provide personalized recommendations based on the doctor's specialty, patient caseload, and clinical interests.

Mobile Accessibility

- Design the dashboard to be responsive and accessible on various devices including smartphones and tablets.
- Develop a dedicated mobile app for convenient access to patient information and test management on the go.
